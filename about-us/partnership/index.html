Partnership

At Global Steel Exports our customers receive a responsive and competitive service for all requests, from supplying single item orders to large contracts. Our global presence ensures goods are sourced in the most cost-effective way to offer value and maximise savings for our clients.

globalnet Procurement

GlobalNet Procurement has been confirmed as our Ghana joint venture distributor. This partnership allows us to utilise our global reach and provide local sourcing and expediting services, reducing our client’s costs with our competitive pricing structure for stock that is not burdened by the implications of taxes. We are proud to be in partnership with a company dedicated to developing the skills of local indigenous Africans in each country they operate within. Something we too are passionate about

Founded in 2018, GlobalNet Procurement is a Ghanaian company which offer products and services locally that will give our sub-Saharan African clients optimum satisfaction and total peace of mind in the knowledge that they are supporting a local business while accessing the global market.
