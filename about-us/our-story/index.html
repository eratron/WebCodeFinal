Our Story

Global Steel Exports Ltd (GSE) was founded in 2016, in London, England. GSE was formed with the aim to provide international procurement services for remote mining and Energy projects. Over the years our company has earned a reputation for excellence and rectitude; with constant attention on striving to do better. At Global Steel Exports we operate in multiple locations throughout the Middle East and Africa. In 2017 we incorporated Global Steel Exports (Pty) Ltd in South Africa. This enables us to provide a complete supply chain solution to our international clients.

As a steel stockist and mill agent, we specialise in exporting metal and plastic products all over the world. Quality products are a priority to us, we only supply high quality products approved by accredited manufacturers, all products are supplied with 3.1 or 2.2 Material Test Certificates. All products can be supplied in a vast range of recognised international standards including BS, ASTM, EN, AISI, and DIN.

With a vast amount contacts internationally, from contractors to on-site personnel, our aim is to act as an extension of your procurement team, giving you access to the global marketplace through our trusted relationships and international partners. With our own warehouse operations in the UK and South Africa, goods are received and carefully inspected by our experienced team before being prepared for delivery to our clients.
